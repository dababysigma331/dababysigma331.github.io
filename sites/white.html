White House

The White House is where the President’s residence and office is located. It was built between October 16, 1792 to November 1, 1800. The White House is located at 1600 Pennsylvania Avenue. Every president has resided here other than George Washington. It was designed by James Hoban in the 1790s.It acts as the President’s home and workplace, and is the headquarters of the President’s staff.
